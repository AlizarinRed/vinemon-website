All Vinemon: Sauce Edition Route 
Encounters and Their Held Items
By: PokefanJulie

****************************************************************************************************
Route 1:

Grass:

LARBY
Item drops:
Common- Oran Berry: It may be used or held by a Vinemon to heal the user by just 10 HP
Uncommon- Bug Gem: A gem with an insect-like essence. When held, it strengthens the power of a Bug-type move only once 
Rare- Royal Jelly: A slick jelly to be held by APIBAE. Allows levitation. Additionally, Honey Slather won’t reduce speed

WEENUT
Item drops:
Common- Oran Berry: It may be used or held by a Vinemon to heal the user by just 10 HP
Uncommon- Sitrus Berry: It may be used or held by a Vinemon to heal the user’s HP a little
Rare- Miracle Seed: An item to be held by a Vinemon. It’s a seed imbued with life-force that boosts the power of Grass-type moves

SKULLSHROOM
Item drops:
Common- Pecha Berry: It may be used or held by a Vinemon to recover from poison
Uncommon- Poison Gem: A gem with an essence of poison. When held, it strengthens the power of a Poison-type move only once
Rare- Funny Spores: A bag of colorful mushroom spores. Powder moves have +1 priority

BELBONIC
Item drops:
Common- Lum Berry: It may be used or held by a Vinemon to recover from any status condition
Uncommon- Dark Gem: A gem with an essence of darkness. When held, it strengthens the power of a Dark-type move only once
Rare- PP Up: It slightly raises the maximum PP of a selected move that has been learned by the target Vinemon

NIDOODLAN (MALE)
Item drops:
Common- Oran Berry: It may be used or held by a Vinemon to heal the user by just 10 HP
Uncommon- Salac Berry: If held by a Vinemon, it raises the Speed stat in a pinch
Rare- Doodle Pen: To be held by a doodled Vinemon. Raises Sp. Atk while also boosting Evasion

NIDOODLAN (FEMALE)
Item drops:
Common- Oran Berry: It may be used or held by a Vinemon to heal the user by just 10 HP
Uncommon- Salac Berry: If held by a Vinemon, it raises the Speed stat in a pinch
Rare- Doodle Pen: To be held by a doodled Vinemon. Raises Sp. Atk while also boosting Evasion

RABBUZZ
Item drops:
Common- Cheri Berry: It may be used or held by a Vinemon to recover from paralysis
Uncommon- Electric Gem: A gem with an essence of electricity. When held, it strengthens the power of a Electric-type move only once
Rare- Light Ball: An item to be held by RABBUZZ. It's a puzzling orb that boosts its Attack and Sp. Atk stats
